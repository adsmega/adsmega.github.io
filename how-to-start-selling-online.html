4 Steps to Start Selling Online Until Successfully Getting Buyers


Imagining online sales is interesting but also scary. On the one hand, we see there are millions of internet users in the world, there is a big market here.

But on the other hand, it seems the way to start is complicated.

Even if there is a step by step that can be followed like folding origami, surely there are many offline sellers who come online.

Here is the guide.

After reading this article, you will know what you can do right now to start selling online.

Selling online now is very easy since there is a marketplace (online market). Such as Amazon, eBay, etc.

5 minutes from now, you can start selling and getting buyers. it's easy, right?

Even some sellers only rely on the marketplace or social media to sell, without creating their website.

Yes, You can... But there is a big weakness behind it.

First, the places of selling are not your own. Supposing you are riding in sales at other people's homes. As a result, your sales can be closed at any time.

Besides, you also look no different from other sellers. This is not good for the long term, because you will not be known.

Second, it is difficult to grow.

I often see people who 100% rely on social media to sell. The business is stable ... it doesn't run out of buyers, but there is no progress.

What is the solution? Use both.

Growing your website takes time, you can use the marketplace and social media to introduce them to your website.

Step # 0 - Don't know what you want to sell?

This is the biggest problem for people who want to do business.
(If you already know what to sell, proceed to step # 1)

The best choice for those who are willing to make a little more effort is making their products. Digital products are not difficult to make as long as you already know what capabilities you can make into products. Or you can also sell services. Other options become a reseller or drop shipper.

You have to solve this problem yourself ... because you know what you want to sell for long-term business.

Step # 1 - Build a sales website foundation

As mentioned earlier, selling at someone else's "home" is not enough. In the short term, it might work ... but not in the long term.

You have to have your website. Need fees ... for hosting and domains. But these costs are nothing compared to the income you will get from sales.

If you start without funds, 0 dollars, no need to directly create a website. Sell your first product/service and then use the money to build a website.

Talk about selling online, usually related to online stores or e-commerce.

Though maybe you don't need an online store.

Most people usually only sell 1-5 products online. If you are also like this, do not create an online store that has a variety of features.

The solution: make 1 page for 1 type of item.

So you make one sales page each for each product. This page contains the title, picture/video, explanation, and how to buy.

But if you have dozens of products, an e-commerce system will be easier.

Once again, if you only sell 1-5 types of products/services. Pages like this are enough:

Step # 2 - Build the foundation of social media

As a seller, of course, we have to invite buyers to come ... for that we will go to a place with many people, social media.

But remember..

Social media is a marketing and communication tool with customers, NOT as your main selling place.

Invite them to your website to buy.

Also, keep in mind that not all products/services can be successfully marketed with social media. Look again at your target market.

Some social media that are commonly used as a means marketing :

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Pinterest
It doesn't have to be all... Even less is better because you can be more focused.

So make sure, where your target market is most active. Then you will also be active on the same social media.

2a. Facebook

This social media seems easy, but actually, it is the most difficult.

Therefore do not be careless. Facebook has a strict spam filter, and you will not be notified if you have been labeled a spammer.

So many people are wasting time on Facebook. This page has more than 27 thousand likes:



But all the posts like this ... no one commented / like / share.

How did it happen?

There are 3 possible causes:
  • Buy LIKE from other people or with FB ads
  • There are no interesting posts
  • Posting too often (optimally 2x a day)
Never buy like! although using the official Facebook Ads.

If you have a lot of followers, but no one has ever interacted with all your posts, then all of your posts will never appear in someone else's News Feed.

Try each of your posts to get lots of likes/shares/comments.

Here's what you can post:
  • Trends or news in industries related to your business
  • Attractive photos with good quality
  • An inspiring story or a pearl of wisdom
  • Product photos - but it must be interesting

2b. Instagram

Here's why Instagram is so popular for businesses:
On Facebook, the engagement rate per post is only around 0.05-1%, while on Instagram it reaches 4.21%. Meaning your photos will get more interactions (in the form of comments/likes). That's why there are lots of active businesses on Instagram.
Tips every time you send photos to Instagram:
  • Photos must be beautiful (if you can't provide good photos, it's better not to use Instagram)
  • Use 5-10 #hashtags that are relevant
  • The optimum number of posts 1-2 times a day (can be more if your photos are interesting)
  • For product photos include a description, price, and how to buy
  • Don't spam other people's unrelated photos
Step # 3 - Get the first sale

Creating a website and social media foundation is easy, the real challenge is getting your first buyer. Not being able to sell means your business will fail.

There are several critical success factors in sales. For example, the price is competitive, the quality is good, the service is friendly.

Let's assume you are the best at these 3 things.

Not guaranteed you can get buyers ... Why?

Because 90% of the market is the same as you. The price of the A and the B is not much different, the quality is equally superior. If so, those three things are no longer relevant ... right?

Low prices, high quality, friendly service ... this is not enough.

So what's more important is how you can appear in front of them, and convince them to buy from you.

In this step, we will discuss how to get buyers for those of you who are just starting to sell online.

One more thing, don't (only) rely on SEO.

You need months to reach the first page. Plus if you only rely on SEO, chances are that you will never succeed. The results of the SEO later will come by itself.

For now, do these steps:

3a. Join the online market

Although I said earlier that you should not take advantage of the marketplace for the long term, in practice it is very effective in getting buyers.

Especially when it's your first time selling. This is because on these websites there are already many people who are ready to buy.

There are 2 conditions in which the marketplace is effective:

  • Its products are commonly sought after (for example iPhone charger)
  • You don't know how to get visitors
Developing your website takes time. New websites cannot be immediately recognized by many people.

Therefore, selling in the marketplace can be the solution while introducing your business and your website. But, remember ... don't 100% rely on the marketplace.

3b. Create content and distribute

There is no doubt that social media and community sites are the best places to bring visitors quickly. But many people fail because the promotion method is wrong.

The problem is that they directly promote links to the homepage or sales page to the community site.

There are 3 types of people on the internet:
  1. Searching for information
  2. Looking for entertainment
  3. Buy products/services
And on community sites, there are only the first and second types. This means that if you put a link about the sale, you will be kicked out, or labeled as a spammer.

But even so, it doesn't mean you can't promote on a community site ... you can, the way you have to change.

Like this:
  • Find a community site that suits your business
  • Look for the main problems of the people there
  • Create content that can be a solution to the problem
  • Distribute
Because most people on the internet want to get information, these strategies are very effective for introducing your business and getting buyers.

Your website will also be easily found through search engines.
